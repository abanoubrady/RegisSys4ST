The "Summer Training Program for Students" is one of the capacity-building programs for students. The program provides practical training on the latest technology trends for students of the Faculty of Computers and Information. It aims to enhance students' abilities and prepare them well to enter the job market.

Overview

Technical Program: AWS Cloud

Prerequisites: Basic Computer Knowledge, General IT technical knowledge, and General IT business knowledge

Exam Name: AWS Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C01)

Technical Program: Cisco CCNA

Prerequisites: Basic computer literacy, Basic PC operating systems navigation skills, and Basic Internet usage skills

Exam Name: Cisco CCNA 200-301

Technical Program: Cyber Ops Associate

Prerequisites: Basic computer literacy, Basic PC operating systems navigation skills, and Basic Internet usage skills

Exam Name: Cisco CBROPS 200-201

Technical Program: Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Prerequisites: Basic knowledge of programming fundamentals

Exam Name: Huawei HCIA-AI H13-311

Objectives

  • Bridge the gap between universities and the labor market requirements
  • Build students' technical and soft skills
  • Spread business culture among students and raise their awareness

Admission Criteria

  • Students who have completed 60 credit hours
  • Students currently enrolled in the Faculties of Computer Science

Agenda

  • Summer vacation period each year: June, July, August, and September

Benefits

  • Offer university students certificates based on attendance stating that they have completed summer training
  • Help students obtain the credit hours required by their faculties
